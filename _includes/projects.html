Mirror Mirror

  • Sep 2023 - May 2024
  • Team size: 12
  • Technologies: Unity, C#, HLSL

Mirror Mirror is a first person puzzle-horror game. Take on the role of a witch, trapped in a bathroom that is haunted by a demon. You must perform the Sealing Ritual to defeat the demon for good, but beware! As you complete the ritual steps, the demon will try to steal power from them and become stronger. Can you complete the ritual before it is too late?

I was responsible for implementing the reflection rendering and many of the puzzle mechanics
